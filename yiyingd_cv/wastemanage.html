Project Overview

In this project, we start from a simple domain topic but nothing else. We carried out secondary research and guerilla research to tackle down the problem scope and target audience of the project. During the investigation, we have found out that a vast amount of construction leftover materials haven’t been fully utilized during the recycling process. A lot of brand new material are going to the landfill directly. Therefore, we made a responsive prototype for Construction Junction, a non-profit warehouse supplying surplus construction materials & appliances at reduced prices. This prototype has two different views for constructor and small construction contractor / household user that allows them to order ideal second-hand materials directly from the site. My role in the project is project manager.

Persona


Small Site Contractor

Needs:

  • Cheaper raw construction material that meets project’s standard
  • On time and accurate delivery service

Site Manager

Needs:

  • Adoption of sustainable site practiceshas a positive impact on how Ryan’s firm is perceived.

CJ Manager

Needs:

  • Wants more customers to buy reusable construction materials from their website. Want publicity of services.
  • That resources are sustainably used.

Sample of Customer Journey Map

This allows us to explore the possible opportunities by aligning the process and discovering pain points.

Solution




The final solution is an e-commerce-like application that face to both large construction site managers and users (including both small site managers and households). This application allows large construction site to cut down their cost on recycling, as now they don’t have to hire a third party but can have construction junction to take away their wastes. The users can have cheaper materials at lower price points. Finally, as the owner of the application, Construction Junction can now have more users and increased profit.

Why Us?


Site Manager

  • Hassle free & low cost disposal of reusable construction material.
  • Conformation to the LEED(Leadership in Energy & Envt Design) standards of building. Which states that 75% of the construction waste should be recycled.

Buyers

  • Good quality construction material at a lower than market price.
  • Custom quantity of material available, bulk orders are not necessary.
  • Preview the materials before hand.

Construction Junction

  • Creates an additional supply of saleable material.
  • Increased sales and profit.
  • But most importantly, by doing this, we are promoting sustainable reuse of material which aligns with the core values of CJ.

Reflection

All in all, the responsive web design project was an experience of great learning right from the research phase to actually designing the product and pitching these brilliant ideas to significant stakeholder (also potential investors).

  • Design shouldn’t be Self Referential: I think the toughest pivot we made in our project was to go with waste management for construction companies instead of the CMU campus. We are glad we did this since we learnt a lot about another domain and designing for them which we would have, otherwise, been unaware of.
  • Conducting Guerilla research in the real world: with the short time we had it seemed even more realistic & we learnt how effective it is in practicality. We also learnt how to identify stakeholder and how to reach out to these people. (email, phone calls, physical interviews)
